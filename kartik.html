When you’re ready to simplify your life with self storage, U-Stor-It Barrio Logan is ready to make your life easier. Our facility is located at 2201 National Ave on the corner of Sampson St and National Ave. It’s located near local eating establishments such as Salud, Border Brewing and Por Vida. With amenities like climate-controlled storage units and high-tech security features, it’s no wonder that U-Stor-It is such a popular storage option for local residents, businesses, and students. Our facility offers a broad range of unit sizes, with a drive-through loading bay to allow you to move in your items in the comfort of air conditioning and makes the moving experience as enjoyable as it can be. There is never a need to worry about parking as you can park inside of our facility when you are moving in. Our security features ensure that all activity is monitored and recorded, providing a well-protected atmosphere for our renters and their stored belongings. Stop by today and let our helpful staff assist you with finding the perfect solution to meet your needs!
